About The Project

Laika Records is not just another vinyl store; it's a passion project born out of a deep love for music. Inspired by the courageous spirit of Laika, the first living creature to journey into space, we aim to explore uncharted musical territories.

Our mission is to blend the timeless charm of vinyl with the convenience of modern technology, making the vinyl experience more accessible and enjoyable for everyone.
